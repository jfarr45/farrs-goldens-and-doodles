Puppies and Pricing

We breed multiple sizes and generations:

  • Petite Goldendoodles (20-25 pounds)
  • Mini/Medium Goldendoodles (25-40 pounds)
  • Standard Goldendoodles (50+ pounds)
  • Mini/Medium Bernedoodles (35-50 pounds)
  • Medium Aussiedoodles (30-45 pounds)
  • Golden Retrievers

Doodle Generations include:

  • F1 is a purebred dog crossed with a poodle, making them 50% of each breed.
  • F1B is an F1 Doodle bred back to a poodle, making their poodle content higher. It averages closer to 75%, but can vary.
  • Multi-Generation is a doodle bred to a doodle.

Deposits and Pricing

Due to the variety of our litters, our prices range from $1,800-$2,500 for goldendoodles, and $3,000 for bernedoodles. This is for puppies placed in pet homes with a spay/neuter contract. Breeding rights and fees can be discussed with approved breeders.

We collect a $200 deposit once we have a confirmed litter and final payment will be arranged at a later time.

We reserve the right to keep puppies from any litter for breeding purposes.
